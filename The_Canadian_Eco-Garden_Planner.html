The Canadian Eco-Garden Planner

An informative guide to creating a low-effort, critter-resistant garden, with recommendations tailored to your region of Canada.

How to Use This Guide

This tool simplifies garden planning. First, select your climatic region from the dropdown menu below. The guide will then load a complete, four-part plan tailored to your area's conditions. Each part of the plan—Foundation, Border, Structure, and Hub—represents a key layer of a successful, low-maintenance garden. Click on any section to expand it and learn about the recommended plant, its benefits, and how to use it effectively.
